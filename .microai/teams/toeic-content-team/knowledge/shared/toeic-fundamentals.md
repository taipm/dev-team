# TOEIC Fundamentals

## Overview

TOEIC (Test of English for International Communication) là bài thi đánh giá khả năng sử dụng tiếng Anh trong môi trường làm việc quốc tế.

## Test Structure

### Listening Section (45 minutes, 100 questions)

| Part | Name | Questions | Description |
|------|------|-----------|-------------|
| 1 | Photographs | 6 | Mô tả hình ảnh |
| 2 | Question-Response | 25 | Hỏi-đáp ngắn |
| 3 | Conversations | 39 | Hội thoại 2-3 người |
| 4 | Talks | 30 | Bài nói chuyên đề |

### Reading Section (75 minutes, 100 questions)

| Part | Name | Questions | Description |
|------|------|-----------|-------------|
| 5 | Incomplete Sentences | 30 | Điền từ vào câu |
| 6 | Text Completion | 16 | Điền từ vào đoạn văn |
| 7 | Reading Comprehension | 54 | Đọc hiểu đoạn văn |

### 2. Grammar Points

**Frequently Tested:**
- Subject-verb agreement
- Tenses (especially present perfect)
- Prepositions
- Relative clauses
- Conditional sentences
- Passive voice
- Gerunds vs infinitives
- Comparatives/superlatives

**Common Traps:**
- Similar-sounding words
- False cognates
- Confusing word forms (noun/verb/adj)
- Phrasal verbs

### 3. Listening Skills

**Key Strategies:**
- Identify main idea quickly
- Note specific details
- Watch for paraphrasing
- Understand implied meaning
- Recognize tone and attitude

**Common Topics:**
- Business meetings
- Phone conversations
- Announcements
- Advertisements
- News reports

## Video Content Guidelines

### Vocabulary Videos

**Structure:**
1. Word introduction (spelling, pronunciation)
2. Definition (simple, clear)
3. Example sentence (business context)
4. Usage tip (common collocations)
5. Practice prompt

**Best Practices:**
- Use IPA pronunciation
- Show word in context
- Highlight common mistakes
- Include synonyms when relevant

### Listening Videos

**Structure:**
1. Context setting
2. Audio clip (or simulated)
3. Key phrases highlight
4. Strategy explanation
5. Practice tips

**Best Practices:**
- Use authentic business scenarios
- Include both American and British accents
- Point out distractors
- Teach active listening techniques

### Grammar Videos

**Structure:**
1. Rule presentation
2. Clear examples
3. Common mistakes
4. Practice exercises
5. Quick reference summary

**Best Practices:**
- Compare similar structures
- Use TOEIC-style examples
- Show wrong vs right
- Provide memory aids
