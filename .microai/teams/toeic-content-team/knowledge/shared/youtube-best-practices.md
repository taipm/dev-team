# YouTube Best Practices

## Algorithm Fundamentals

### Key Metrics

| Metric | Importance | Description |
|--------|------------|-------------|
| Watch Time | Critical | Total minutes watched |
| CTR | High | Click-through rate from impressions |
| Retention | High | % of video watched |
| Engagement | Medium | Likes, comments, shares |
| Subscribers | Medium | New subs from video |

### Algorithm Signals

**Positive Signals:**
- High watch time
- Strong retention curve
- Good CTR (4-10%+)
- Engagement within 24 hours
- Session time (viewers watch more)

**Negative Signals:**
- High bounce rate
- Low retention
- Dislike ratio
- Spam comments
- Misleading thumbnails

## Shorts vs Standard

### YouTube Shorts

**Specifications:**
- Duration: 15-60 seconds
- Aspect: 9:16 (vertical)
- Resolution: 1080x1920
- No end screens
- No cards

**Best Practices:**
- Hook in first 1-2 seconds
- Fast-paced content
- Clear, readable text
- One concept per video
- Strong CTA at end

**Algorithm Notes:**
- Separate algorithm from long-form
- Viral potential higher
- Lower RPM but high volume
- Cross-platform (TikTok, Reels)

### Standard Videos

**Specifications:**
- Duration: 3-10 minutes optimal
- Aspect: 16:9 (horizontal)
- Resolution: 1920x1080
- End screens: last 20 seconds
- Cards: throughout

**Best Practices:**
- Hook in first 15 seconds
- Pattern interrupts every 30-60s
- Clear structure with timestamps
- Strong thumbnail + title combo
- CTA for subscribe/like

**Algorithm Notes:**
- Watch time weighted heavily
- Retention curve critical
- Higher RPM potential
- Better for building authority

## SEO Optimization

### Title Optimization

**Formula:**
`[Primary Keyword] + [Benefit/Hook] + [Identifier]`

**Guidelines:**
- Under 60 characters
- Keyword at start
- Numbers perform well
- Questions create curiosity
- Avoid clickbait

**Examples:**
✓ "10 TOEIC Words You Must Know | Part 5 Vocabulary"
✓ "TOEIC Listening Part 3: Complete Strategy Guide"
✗ "You Won't BELIEVE These TOEIC Tips!!!"

### Description Optimization

**Structure:**
```
Line 1-2: Hook + Value (visible in search)
Line 3-5: Summary with keywords
Line 6+: Timestamps
Line X: Key takeaways
Line Y: Related links
Line Z: CTA
Final: Hashtags (3-5)
```

**Keywords:**
- Natural integration
- Primary keyword in first 25 words
- 2-3 keyword variations
- Don't stuff

### Tags Strategy

**Tag Order:**
1. Primary keyword (exact)
2. Primary keyword variations
3. Broad category tags
4. Related topic tags
5. Channel name

**Guidelines:**
- 8-15 tags optimal
- Mix specific and broad
- Include misspellings
- Use tag research tools

## Thumbnail Design

### Best Practices

**Elements:**
- Large, readable text (3-4 words max)
- High contrast colors
- Face/emotion when possible
- Clear at small sizes
- Brand consistency

**Technical:**
- Size: 1280x720
- Format: JPG or PNG
- Under 2MB

**For Educational Content:**
- Numbers (e.g., "5 Tips", "990")
- Before/After
- Problem statement
- "Secret" or "Tip" badges
- TOEIC score indicators

## Engagement Tactics

### Hooks

**Types:**
- Question hook: "Did you know...?"
- Problem hook: "Struggling with...?"
- Promise hook: "In 60 seconds, you'll..."
- Curiosity hook: "The secret to..."
- Controversy hook: "Everyone gets this wrong..."

### Calls to Action

**Placement:**
- Start: "Hit subscribe for daily TOEIC tips"
- Middle: "Drop a comment if you knew this"
- End: "Like if this helped!"

**Types:**
- Subscribe + bell
- Like
- Comment (specific question)
- Share
- Watch next video
