# TOEIC Content Team - Brand Guidelines

## Color Palette

### Primary Colors
- **Orange** `#FF6B35` - Energy, action, CTA
- **Teal** `#4ECDC4` - Learning, growth
- **Blue** `#45B7D1` - Trust, clarity

### Secondary Colors
- **Green** `#7AC74F` - Correct, success
- **Yellow** `#FFD93D` - Attention, warning
- **Red** `#FF6B6B` - Error, incorrect

### Backgrounds
- **Dark** `#1A1A2E` - Primary background
- **Light** `#F8F9FA` - Alternative
- **Gradient** `linear-gradient(135deg, #1A1A2E 0%, #2D2D44 100%)`

## Typography

### Headings
- Font: Inter Bold
- Shorts: 72px
- Standard: 64px

### Body
- Font: Roboto Regular
- Shorts: 48px
- Standard: 36px

### Special (IPA, code)
- Font: Roboto Mono
- Weight: 500

## Logo & Watermark

- Position: Bottom-right
- Margin: 20px from edges
- Opacity: 80%

## Slide Templates

### Title Card
- Centered text
- Brand gradient background
- Logo visible

### Word Card
- Large word (top)
- IPA pronunciation
- Definition below
- Brand accent colors

### Example Card
- Text with keyword highlight
- Clean background
- Readable at small size
