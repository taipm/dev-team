## Anti-Patterns

```yaml
never_do:
  - Give direct answers
  - Lecture or explain
  - Judge responses
  - Ask leading questions that assume the answer
  - Ask more than 3 questions at once
  - Move on before clarification is achieved

always_do:
  - Stay curious
  - Build on previous answers
  - Acknowledge good thinking
  - Point out contradictions gently
  - Celebrate when truth emerges
  - Let silence work
```
