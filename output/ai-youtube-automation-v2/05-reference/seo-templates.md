# SEO Templates

## Muc dich
Templates chuan cho Title, Description, Tags de toi uu SEO YouTube.

---

## Title Templates

### Formula 1: Number + Benefit
```
[Number] + [Action/Thing] + [Benefit/Result]
```
Examples:
- "5 Thoi Quen Buoi Sang Giup Ban Thanh Cong"
- "7 Ky Nang Bat Buoc Phai Co Nam 2026"
- "10 Cach Kiem Tien Online Khong Can Von"

### Formula 2: How-To
```
Cach + [Action] + [Result/Benefit] + (Optional: Timeframe)
```
Examples:
- "Cach Doc 50 Cuon Sach Moi Nam"
- "Cach Hoc Nhanh Gap 3 Lan Binh Thuong"
- "Cach Thuc Day 5h Sang Ma Khong Met"

### Formula 3: Question
```
Tai Sao + [Subject] + [Action/State]?
```
Examples:
- "Tai Sao Nguoi Giau Day Luc 5h Sang?"
- "Tai Sao 95% Nguoi That Bai Voi Muc Tieu?"
- "Tai Sao Nao Ghet Su Thay Doi?"

### Formula 4: Curiosity Gap
```
[Surprising Statement] + (Hook)
```
Examples:
- "Bi Mat Cua Nhung Nguoi IQ Cao"
- "Su That Ve Da Nhiem It Nguoi Biet"
- "Dieu Nay Se Thay Doi Cuoc Doi Ban"

## Tag Strategy

### Primary Tags (3)
Exact match keywords cho video
- Main topic exact match
- Long-tail variation 1
- Long-tail variation 2

### Related Tags (5)
Broader topics lien quan
- Parent category
- Related concept 1
- Related concept 2
- Trending related term
- Competitor channel topic

### Channel Tags (2)
Branding tags
- Channel name
- Niche identifier

### Trending Tags (5)
Current trending topics (update weekly)
- Trending topic 1
- Trending topic 2
- Seasonal term
- News-related term
- Pop culture reference

## Hashtag Guidelines

### Placement
- **In title**: 0 (khong dung trong title)
- **In description**: 3-5 (dau description)
- **Max hashtags**: 15 (qua nhieu bi spam)

### Best Practices
1. **Relevant** - Chi dung hashtags lien quan
2. **Specific** - #HocNhanh vs #Hoc (specific tot hon)
3. **Mix** - Ket hop niche + trending hashtags
4. **Vietnamese** - Dung tieng Viet cho Vietnamese audience

## Keyword Research Process

### Step 1: Seed Keywords
1. Brainstorm main topics
2. Use YouTube search autocomplete
3. Check competitor video titles

### Step 2: Expansion
1. YouTube autocomplete variations
2. Related searches
3. TubeBuddy/VidIQ suggestions

### Step 3: Validation
1. Check search volume (TubeBuddy)
2. Competition analysis
3. Trending score

### Step 4: Selection
1. Pick 1 primary keyword
2. Pick 2-3 secondary keywords
3. Create title around primary
