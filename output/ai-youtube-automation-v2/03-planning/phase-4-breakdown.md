## Tuan 19-20: Ad Revenue Optimization

### Video Ad Settings
| Ad Type | Placement | Revenue Impact |
|---------|-----------|----------------|
| Pre-roll | Auto | High |
| Mid-roll | Manual (8+ min) | Medium |
| Post-roll | Auto | Low |
| Display | Always on | Low |

### RPM Optimization
- **Longer videos** (8-15 min) = More mid-rolls
- **Better retention** = Higher ad completion
- **Quality content** = Better advertiser match
- Target RPM: $2-5 USD

## Tuan 23-24: Sponsorship Outreach

### Sponsor Tiers
| Tier | Subscribers | Rate |
|------|-------------|------|
| Micro | 1K-10K | $50-200/video |
| Small | 10K-50K | $200-500/video |
| Medium | 50K-100K | $500-2000/video |

### Outreach Strategy
1. **Create** media kit (stats, demographics)
2. **List** target sponsors (relevant to niche)
3. **Email** outreach template
4. **Negotiate** terms
5. **Deliver** sponsored content

### Email Template
```
Subject: Partnership Opportunity - [Channel Name]

Hi [Name],

I run [Channel Name], a Vietnamese educational channel
with [X] subscribers focusing on [niche].

I'd love to discuss a potential partnership...

[Stats: Views, Engagement, Demographics]

Best,
[Your Name]
```
