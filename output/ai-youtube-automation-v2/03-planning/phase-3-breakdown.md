## Tuan 11-12: Analytics Tracking

### Google Sheets Dashboard
| Metric | Formula | Update |
|--------|---------|--------|
| Views/Video | =SUM(Views)/COUNT(Videos) | Weekly |
| Watch Time % | =AvgWatchTime/VideoLength | Weekly |
| Sub Growth | =NewSubs-LostSubs | Weekly |
| Revenue Est | =RPM*Views/1000 | Monthly |

### Key KPIs
- CTR Target: >8%
- Watch Time: >60%
- Sub/View Ratio: >2%
